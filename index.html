Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: October 21, 2025

What is KidSpeak?

KidSpeak is a fun language-learning app designed for children, combining safe AI conversations with kid-friendly lessons.

How do I get started?

Open the app, choose a topic, and start interacting with the lesson or AI conversation. You can adjust settings from the Settings page anytime.

Do I need an account to use KidSpeak?

You can explore without sign up or login. Some features may require a subscription to unlock full access.

Which devices are supported?

KidSpeak supports most modern Android and iOS devices. For the best experience, keep your device and the app updated to the latest versions.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. We take privacy seriously and follow industry-standard security practices. For details, see the Privacy Policy below.

Does KidSpeak work offline?

Some lessons may work offline, but AI conversations and certain content require an internet connection.

How can I manage or cancel a subscription?

On iOS, manage from your Apple ID subscriptions. On Android, manage from Google Play subscriptions. You can also access these from the app's Settings > Manage Subscription.

How do I report a problem or share feedback?

Go to Settings > Contact Us to email our support team. Please include details and screenshots if possible so we can help quickly.

How often is new content added?

We regularly add new topics and improvements. Keep the app updated to receive the latest features and lessons.

Where can I learn more about KidSpeak's features?

Explore the app's Settings, check the help sections, and follow in-app prompts for tips. We're continually improving the experience for kids and parents.
